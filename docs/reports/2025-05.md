# Bitcoin Educational Content - Monthly Activity Report
## May 2025

### Executive Summary

May 2025 was an exceptionally productive month for the Bitcoin Educational Content repository with **96 commits** across **47 pull requests**. The month demonstrated strong focus on infrastructure maintenance (22.9%), educational content proofreading (18.8%), and course development (18.8%). Key highlights include the launch of PHI301 (Philosophy course), significant wallet tutorial expansion, and continued emphasis on multilingual accessibility.

**Key Metrics:**
- **96 total commits** (13% increase from typical monthly average)
- **47 pull requests** merged successfully
- **22 new projects** added to the ecosystem
- **12 new tutorials** published
- **18 proofreading initiatives** completed across multiple languages
- **3 major course updates** including new PHI301 philosophy course
